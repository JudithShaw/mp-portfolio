Video Production

Location

For the STREET TALK video we interviewed people in and around Alexander Park in Moss Side, UK. We wanted to get their opinion about the current state of football, especially after the relaxing of Covid19 restrictions. The interview style was light-hearted and upbeat and we tried to be inclusive in the range of contributors.

Production Team

Jakob - Camera operator, Damon - Presenter/Editor, Humayra - Presenter, Nuria - Director, Judith - Production.

Project Management

It was important for the team to manage their time in the production schedule. We used TRELLO to organise ideas. We conducted a risk assesment, location recce and equipment checklist ahead of shooting the piece. On the day of the shoot we gained consent from the participants. The team adhered to Covid19 protocol at all times.

Broadcast Format

The piece was filmed on a JVC 4k camera in a widescreen, landscape format - 16:9 aspect ratio which is a more formal format used in television. The footage was captured handheld to make it appear fast-paced and less formal. It was edited in DaVinci.

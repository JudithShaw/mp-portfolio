Background

THE CIRCLE OF LIFE

Looking at the subject of the HUMAN EXISTANCE the theme for the 5 images is THE CIRCLE OF LIFE. This is a symbolic representation of birth, survival and death. It is nature's way of taking and giving back life to the earth - representing the infinite nature of energy.

Dawn

This image of a pregnant woman represents the beginning of the cycle/circle of life. Three-point lighting was used to create the feeling of a ‘new dawn’ representing pregnancy in the first photograph. It was shot in a studio and the effects were enhanced in photoshop by increasing the contrast and then adding a lens flare.

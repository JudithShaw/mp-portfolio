Photography Basics


Different lighting styles was used in creating the 5 photographs to represent The Circle of Life.

Three-point lighting was used to create the feeling of a ‘new dawn’ representing pregnancy in the first photograph. It was shot in a studio and the effects were enhanced in photoshop by increasing the contrast and then adding a lens flare.

The second and third photographs were taken outdoors with natural lighting and edited in post-production to create artistic effects.

The fourth photograph was taken indoors with natural lighting – adjusting the iso and aperture to the desirable setting.

The fifth photograph was a screenshot from a video. It was taken in natural lighting and enhanced in photoshop by increasing the contrast and adding a lens flare.
